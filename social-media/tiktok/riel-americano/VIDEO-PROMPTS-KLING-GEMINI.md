```
Slow motion extreme close-up of a skilled elderly craftsman's weathered hands

machine. Soft natural light entering from a factory window at a 45-degree angle,
casting gentle shadows on the fabric texture. Documentary film style, desaturated
warm tones, slight grain. No camera movement — locked tripod shot. 8 seconds.
Cinematic 4K vertical 9:16.
```

```
Documentary-style vertical video (9:16). Close-up shot of aged, experienced hands
working with white fabric on an old sewing machine in a textile factory. Natural
side lighting from a window creates soft shadows. Film grain, muted warm palette,
black and white with slight sepia tint. Slow deliberate hand movements. Static
camera. Duration: 8-10 seconds. No music, no text.
```

## TIPS DE PRODUCCIÓN

### Flujo recomendado (CapCut)
1. Generá el video con Kling/Veo
2. Abrí el HTML en el browser → `F12` → Comment out el div `.photo`
3. Screenshot de pantalla completa → guardá como PNG
4. En CapCut: video abajo (layer 1) → PNG overlay arriba (layer 2)
5. Ajustá posición del overlay para que el gradiente tape bien los bordes
6. Exportá 1080×1920 a 30fps

### Duración sugerida por pieza
- Pieza 01 Humanización: 8-10 seg — ritmo lento, documental
- Pieza 02 Innovación: 7-9 seg — más rítmico, tech
- Pieza 03 Diseño: 10-12 seg — viaje cinematográfico

### Música (TikTok Library)
- Pieza 01: búsca "piano documental" o "cinematic ambient" en TikTok sounds
- Pieza 02: "minimal tech" o "clean electronic"
- Pieza 03: "luxury ambient" o "architecture film score"
